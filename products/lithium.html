LITHIUM BATTERY

Lithium Ion batteries are used commonly in many portable electronic devices such as laptops and cell phones, as well as in electric vehicles. As compared to conventional batteries, LI batteries bring a higher voltage density, lower self-discharge rate, and higher voltage capacity. The high current capacity in LI batteries also makes them a vital component in large UPS systems. ProstarM offers best quality made in India Lithium Ion battery UPS systems. Owing to the LI batteries, these UPS systems have a longer lifespan and charge faster than other average UPS systems.

Features of LI Batteries

  • Safe and easy to recharge
  • Faster charging
  • Long lifespan
  • Low maintenance requirements

Types of LI Batteries

Lithium Iron Phosphate (“LFP”, LiFePO4)

Lithium Iron Magnesium Phosphate (LiFeMgPO4)

Lithium Manganese Oxide (“LMO”, LiMn2O4)

Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt
